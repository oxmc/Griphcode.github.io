Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker (2013)

Probably what we're most well-known for.

The archetypal idle game where you click cookies and deal with deranged grandmothers.

Has been described as dangerously addictive.

Cookie Clicker classic

Cookie Clicker classic (2013)

The simple, original version where it all started.

Click on a big cookie for hours of mindless fun!

(We won't judge, promise.)

Neverending Legacy

Neverending Legacy (2017)

[Alpha]

Half-idle game, half-strategy game; lead your people from a tribe of starving cavemen to a thriving civilization!

Nested

Nested (2011)

A strange universe-expanding game that's been described as "whoah dude".

Possibly Orteil's favorite thing he's ever made.

Explore procedural galaxies, land on unknown planets, read the thoughts of every living thing, rummage through people's pockets, and more!

Genesis

Genesis (2010)

A peaceful game about combining elements. You start with earth, water, fire and air, and end up with rainbows and butterflies and such.

A bit bad, a bit unfinished.

Requires Flash. Hosted on Newgrounds.com.

Murder Games

Murder Games (2018)

Automated survival simulator.

Create a cast of characters, then watch them betray and slaughter each other round after round until one team or one person survives.

RandomGen

RandomGen (2014)

Create and share random generators using a simple syntax.

The possibilities are limitless!

Idle Game Maker

Idle Game Maker (2017)

A site where you can make your own idle games just by writing a text file.

Comes with copious documentation.

May be used to create other kinds of games if you're creative.

Video Game Generator

Video Game Generator (2012)

Generates a random idea for a game, for devs struggling with writer's block.

Untick the "Sanity" box to get much less predictable results.

Turtle Toy

Turtle Toy (2011)

A simple implementation of L-systems.

Generate and create wacky fractal doohickeys.

Life

Life (2012)

A simple implementation of Conway's Game of Life, featuring custom rules and random rule generation.
